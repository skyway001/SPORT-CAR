CRICKET


  • Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players each.
  • The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field with a rectangular 22-yard-long pitch in the center.
  • Each team has eleven players, including a captain, and they take turns playing offense (batting) and defense (fielding).
